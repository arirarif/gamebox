Right of Withdrawal

As a consumer within the European Union, you have the right to withdraw from the purchase contract within 14 days without giving any reason, in accordance with EU Directive 2011/83/EU and the German Civil Code (BGB §§ 355 ff.).

The withdrawal period begins on the day you or a third party designated by you (other than the carrier) receives the goods. For orders with multiple shipments, the period begins when you receive the last item.

To exercise your right of withdrawal:

  • Send a clear declaration of your intention to withdraw (e.g., by email to support@gamebox.de or by post) before the 14-day period expires.
  • You may use our withdrawal form template, but this is not mandatory.
  • It is sufficient that the withdrawal notice is sent before the deadline expires.

Exceptions: The right of withdrawal does not apply to the following:

  • Goods that have been personalized or custom-made according to your specifications (e.g., custom-engraved items, personalized name labels).
  • Sealed goods that are not suitable for return due to hygiene reasons, if the seal has been removed after delivery.

Upon valid withdrawal, we will reimburse all payments received from you, including standard delivery costs, within 14 days of receiving your withdrawal notice. We may withhold the refund until we have received the returned goods or proof of return shipment.
