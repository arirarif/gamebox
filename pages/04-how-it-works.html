Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I see what’s inside before buying?

Each product page shows a detailed description of what’s included. The exact items may vary slightly to keep an element of surprise, but you’ll always know the theme and quantity.

Q: Can I ship directly to the recipient?

Absolutely! During checkout you can enter a different shipping address. We’ll include your greeting card inside and won’t include any pricing information.

Q: What if my child doesn’t like it?

We offer a 30-day return policy. If the box is unopened and in its original packaging, you can return it for a full refund or exchange it for a different theme.

Q: Are the toys safe for young children?

All our products are CE certified and EN 71 compliant. We use non-toxic materials and ensure every item is age-appropriate for the listed age range.

Q: How long does shipping take?

Standard delivery within Germany takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for an additional fee. We also ship EU-wide.

Q: Can I create a fully custom box?

You can customize the size, color theme, and add extras. For fully bespoke boxes (e.g., corporate gifts or events), please contact us for a custom quote.
