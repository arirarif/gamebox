Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order? ▼
Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking email with a link to follow your package in real time. You can also check your order status in the My Account section.
Can I change or cancel my order? ▼
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placing them. After that, the order enters our fulfillment process. Please contact us as soon as possible.
Do you offer corporate / bulk orders? ▼
Yes! We offer custom bulk orders for events, corporate gifts, and special occasions. Please use the contact form above and select “Custom / Corporate Order” as the subject.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Klarna (pay later / installments), and bank transfer (Sofortüberweisung). All payments are processed securely via SSL encryption.
How do returns work? ▼
We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Returns within Germany are free. See our Returns & Refunds page for full details.
Do you ship internationally? ▼
We currently ship to all EU countries. Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days depending on destination. Express options are available for most countries.
