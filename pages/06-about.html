Our Story

Making Every Gift Unforgettable

GameBox was born from a simple idea: gift-giving for kids should be joyful, easy, and meaningful. As parents ourselves, we knew the struggle of finding the perfect present — something creative, high-quality, and exciting to unwrap.

So in 2020, we created GameBox — curated themed gift boxes packed with building blocks, creative supplies, and fun extras, all wrapped in beautifully designed packaging. Every box is a complete experience, not just a gift.

Based in Germany, we hand-select every item and partner with trusted European manufacturers to ensure the highest quality and safety standards. Today, we’ve brought joy to over 10,000 happy kids across Europe.

🎯

Our Mission

To make gift-giving effortless and magical by creating themed boxes that spark creativity, inspire play, and deliver unforgettable unboxing experiences for children.

🔮

Our Vision

To become Europe’s most loved gift box brand — where every box tells a story, every item has a purpose, and every child feels the magic of a thoughtfully curated gift.

How we got here

Our Journey

2020

The Idea Is Born

Markus has the idea for themed gift boxes for kids after struggling to find the perfect birthday present for his son.

2021

First Box Collection

GameBox launches with 3 themes: Construction, Dinosaurs, and Space. The first 500 boxes sell out in weeks.

2022

Growing the Range

We expand to 8 themes and add the customizable box builder. The team grows to 10 people.

2023

EU-Wide Shipping

GameBox starts shipping across the European Union. We reach 5,000 happy customers.

2024

Gift Finder & Add-ons

Launch of the interactive Gift Finder tool and expanded add-on accessories. Partnerships with European toy makers.

2025–Now

10,000+ Happy Kids

We cross the 10,000 customer milestone and continue innovating with new themes and seasonal collections.
