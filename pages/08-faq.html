Ordering

How do I place an order?▼
Browse our shop, select a themed box, customize it (size, color, add-ons), add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay with credit card, PayPal, or Klarna.
Can I change or cancel my order?▼
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placing them. After that, the order enters fulfillment. Contact us immediately at support@gamebox.de.
Is there a minimum order amount?▼
No minimum order is required. However, free shipping is available on orders over $150. Standard shipping fees apply to smaller orders.
Can I order as a guest without an account?▼
Yes, guest checkout is available. However, creating an account lets you track orders, save wishlists, and reorder faster.
🚚

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?▼
Standard delivery within Germany: 3–5 business days. EU delivery: 5–7 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for an additional fee.
Do you ship internationally?▼
We currently ship to all EU countries. We’re working on expanding to other regions. Check our Shipping page for the full list.
How can I track my order?▼
Once shipped, you’ll receive a tracking email with a link. You can also check order status in your My Account dashboard.
Can I ship to a different address (as a gift)?▼
Absolutely! Enter a different shipping address at checkout. We’ll include your greeting card and won’t include any pricing information in the package.
🔃

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?▼
We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. See our Returns page for full details.
How do I initiate a return?▼
Contact us at support@gamebox.de with your order number. We’ll send you a return label and instructions. Returns within Germany are free.
When will I receive my refund?▼
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item. The refund goes back to your original payment method.
🎁

Products & Customization

What’s inside a GameBox?▼
Each box contains themed items like building blocks, mini figures, stickers, activity booklets, and fun extras. The exact contents depend on the theme and size you choose.
Are the toys safe for children?▼
All products are CE certified and EN 71 compliant. We use non-toxic materials and ensure every item is age-appropriate for the listed age range.
Can I create a fully custom box?▼
You can customize size, color theme, and add extras. For fully bespoke boxes (corporate gifts, events), contact us for a custom quote.
Do you offer corporate / bulk orders?▼
Yes! We offer volume discounts for bulk orders. Contact us via the Contact page with your requirements and we’ll provide a custom quote.
💳

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Klarna (pay later / installments), and Sofortüberweisung (instant bank transfer). All payments are SSL-encrypted.
Is it safe to pay on your website?▼
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI-compliant payment processing. Your payment details are never stored on our servers.
Can I pay in installments?▼
Yes, through Klarna you can split your payment into 3 interest-free installments, or pay within 30 days.
👤

My Account

How do I create an account?▼
Click the account icon in the header or visit the My Account page. You can register with your email and create a password, or sign in during checkout.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?▼
On the login page, click “Forgot Password?” and enter your email. You’ll receive a link to create a new password within minutes.
How do I delete my account?▼
Contact us at support@gamebox.de and we’ll process your account deletion request within 48 hours, in compliance with GDPR regulations.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is happy to help. Reach out and we’ll respond within 24 hours.
