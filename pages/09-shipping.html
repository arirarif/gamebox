Good to know

Important Information

⏰ Order Cut-Off Times ▼
Orders placed before 2:00 PM CET on business days are processed the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends and public holidays will be processed the next business day. Express orders follow the same cut-off — order before 2 PM for next-day delivery. Same-day delivery in Berlin requires ordering before 12:00 PM.
📦 Packaging & Presentation ▼
Every GameBox is shipped in a sturdy outer carton to protect the beautifully designed gift box inside. We use eco-friendly, recyclable packaging materials. If you’re sending the box as a gift, rest assured — no pricing information or invoices are included in the package. A gift receipt can be accessed through your account.
🌍 Customs & Import Duties (Non-EU) ▼
Shipments to Switzerland and Norway may be subject to local customs duties, import taxes, or brokerage fees. These charges are determined by your country’s customs authority and are the responsibility of the recipient. GameBox has no control over these charges and cannot predict their amount. We recommend checking with your local customs office before ordering.
📝 Address Changes & Failed Deliveries ▼
Need to change your shipping address? Contact us at support@gamebox.de as soon as possible. Address changes are possible within 2 hours of placing your order, before it enters fulfillment. After a package has shipped, we cannot modify the destination. If a delivery fails (incorrect address, unclaimed), the package will be returned to us and we’ll contact you to arrange reshipment (additional shipping fees may apply).

Still Have Questions About Shipping?

Our support team is here to help with any delivery questions. Reach out and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours.
