Before you return

Return Eligibility

Please review the following conditions to ensure your item qualifies for a return.

✓

What CAN Be Returned

  • ✓ Items that are unused and in original condition — no signs of wear, play, or damage.
  • ✓ Products in their original packaging with all tags, seals, and accessories included.
  • ✓ Orders returned within 30 days of the delivery date (postmark date counts).
  • ✓ Items that arrived damaged or defective — contact us immediately with photos.
  • ✓ Wrong item received — we’ll send the correct one and cover return shipping.
✗

What CANNOT Be Returned

  • ✗ Opened or used items — boxes that have been unsealed, assembled, or played with.
  • ✗ Personalized or custom items — boxes with custom greeting cards or bespoke configurations.
  • ✗ Sale items priced under $5 — deeply discounted clearance items are final sale.
  • ✗ Gift cards and digital products — these are non-refundable once issued.
  • ✗ Items returned after 30 days — the return window has closed after this period.

Common questions

Returns & Refunds FAQ

Can I return a box that has already been opened? ▼
Unfortunately, opened or used items cannot be returned for a refund. However, if the product arrived damaged or defective, please contact us within 7 days with photos and we’ll arrange a replacement or full refund regardless of whether it has been opened.
Do I have to pay for return shipping? ▼
Returns within Germany are completely free — we provide a prepaid DHL return label. For returns from other EU countries, a flat-rate return shipping fee of €4.95 will be deducted from your refund amount. If the return is due to our error (wrong or defective item), return shipping is always free.
How long does it take to process my refund? ▼
Once we receive and inspect your returned item, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. The refund goes back to your original payment method. Please note that your bank may take an additional 2–3 business days to reflect the credit on your statement. Store credit refunds are applied instantly.
Can I exchange my box for a different theme or size? ▼
Yes! We’re happy to exchange your unopened box for a different theme, size, or color variant. Simply contact us to initiate the exchange. We’ll ship the replacement as soon as we receive your return. If there’s a price difference, we’ll charge or refund the difference accordingly.

Need Help with a Return?

Our support team is here to make the process as smooth as possible. Reach out and we’ll respond within 24 hours.
